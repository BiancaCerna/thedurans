A Letter

Dear Friends
& Family,

When we first started dreaming about our wedding, we kept coming back to one thought: we wanted it to be about more than just one day. We wanted it to be an opportunity to celebrate and pour into the people who have poured into us throughout the last decade of our lives.

We couldn’t think of a better way to do that than by sharing a new experience together. Our hope was that our wedding would feel like a family vacation — filled with adventure, laughter, meaningful conversations, and memories we’d all carry home long after the weekend ended.

Looking back, we couldn’t have imagined anything more beautiful. From the rain gently drifting through the temple ruins to dancing and singing along to our favorite songs surrounded by the people we love most, the entire weekend felt magical. There were countless smiles, so many happy tears, and more love than we could have ever hoped for.

We are so deeply grateful that you chose to make the journey to Guatemala. The miles you traveled, the time you gave — it meant everything. Your presence was the greatest gift we could have ever received, and your love is something we will carry into every chapter ahead.

With all our love and gratitude,Bianca & Anthony
